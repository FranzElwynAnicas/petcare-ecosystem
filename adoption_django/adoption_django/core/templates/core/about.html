About Pawsitive Manila

We're on a mission to transform pet adoption in the Philippines by connecting loving pets with responsible owners through a compassionate, transparent, and efficient process.

Our Mission

Promote kindness and provide easier access to pets in need of care, love, and a forever home.

Our Community

We partner with shelters and volunteers to create a safe and transparent adoption process.

Our Promise

Every pet listed on our platform has been properly checked, vaccinated, and evaluated for adoption.

Why Choose Pawsitive Manila?

Fast & Easy Process

Streamlined adoption application with quick approval times

Verified Profiles

All shelters and pet profiles are thoroughly verified

Expert Support

Live chat with experienced adoption counselors

Health Transparency

Complete medical and behavioral history for each pet

Post-Adoption Care

Continued guidance and resources for new pet owners

Community Recognition

Award-winning platform trusted by thousands
